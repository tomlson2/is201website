Devoted To Faith

I served a two year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Bolivia and Orem. I decided to leave my family and friends for two years because I knew it would give me the opportunity to help so many other people! While I was there I learned so many things, but most importantly I learned the importance of faith and understanding of our Savior Jesus Christ.
